Bio



My academic background is heavy in math, modeling, and research. During my Master's, I taught at the UW Emerging Technologies lab (Android, VR in Unity3D, Robotics) and researched blockchain, cryptography, and secure hardware design at the UW Blockchain Lab.

Since then, I've worked at startups effectively applying enterprise-grade AI/ML and blockchain. At SturdyAI, we reduced millions of B2B communications down to actionable signals; I worked with their Postgres databases, Python backend, and AI/ML with Python libraries.

Next, TruckCoinSwap put me through the meatgrinder of tech startups. The token required deep dives into finance and economics and I fleshed out my full-stack as we built our app. OCR, blur detection, and image processing power TCS's pipeline. I've earned an education in marketing, biz dev, venture capital, management, and more from the school of hard knocks (and fantastic mentors).

I'm currently using Python, FastAPI, Prometheus metrics, Grafana, and LangChain to build a scalable backend interface to OpenAI's API for integrating GPT-4/ChatGPT in applications. I'm also enrolled in Databrick's EDX courses on LLM applications to solidify my skills. Read about them here.
